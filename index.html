Pharmacist reviews that are
- Bespoke for ICB/ PCN/ GP Practices
- Quality assured


Precision Meds specialises in pharmacist reviews, focusing on delivering clinical excellence
as well as cost and capacity savings for the NHS.

Our pharmacists maintain the highest standard, and are backed at all times by live Senior
Pharmacist supervision. They are kept updated through regular training and teaching seminars,
and are available to provide face-to-face or remote reviews.

Precision Meds offers ICB's, PCN's and GP practices full control of the scope of the pharmacist
reviews and of how communication is handled between themselves and the pharmacists.

Precision Meds provides a range of pharmacy reviews, whether for:
- patient groups based on condition, such as those with asthma or diabetes
- other patient groups that the ICB, PCN or GP practice might wish to focus on
- overall medication optimisation
